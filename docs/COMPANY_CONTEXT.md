# Norvalt - Company Overview

## Mission
Transform how European car dealerships manage customer relationships by providing an AI-powered platform that ensures no lead is ever lost and every customer receives instant, personalized attention 24/7.

## Value Proposition
**"The only platform Norwegian car dealerships need to capture, respond to, and convert leads from all sources - powered by AI"**

### Core Promise
- **Instant Response:** AI responds to every lead within 90 seconds (vs industry average 4+ hours)
- **Unified System:** All leads from all channels in one dashboard
- **24/7 Coverage:** Never lose a lead outside business hours
- **Norwegian-First:** Built for Norwegian market with local language support
- **Automotive-Specific:** Deep understanding of dealer workflows and pain points

## Core Offerings

### Phase 1: Lead Management Platform (MVP - Current Focus)
1. **Multi-Source Lead Capture**
   - Website contact forms
   - Email monitoring (importer portals: Toyota.no, VW.no, etc.)
   - Facebook Lead Ads
   - Manual lead entry

2. **AI-Powered Auto-Response**
   - Instant email/SMS replies in Norwegian
   - Context-aware responses based on vehicle interest
   - Human handoff when needed
   - 24/7 availability

3. **Unified Lead Inbox**
   - All leads in one dashboard
   - Customer profile cards
   - Conversation history
   - Lead status tracking

4. **Basic Automation**
   - Sales rep notifications (SMS/email)
   - Follow-up sequences (Day 1, 3, 7)
   - Task reminders

### Phase 2: Expanded Features (Post-MVP)
- Advanced inventory management

- Email & sms marketing automation
