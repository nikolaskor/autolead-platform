# Norvalt Customer Profiles & Market Insights

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Initial Primary Target: Norwegian Car Dealerships

**Company Characteristics:**
- **Size:** 10-200 employees
- **Sales Volume:** 50-300 cars per year

- **Brands:** All automotive brands (Toyota, VW, Nissan, Audi, Tesla, etc.)

**Decision-Making Unit:**
- **Economic Buyer:** General Manager or Dealership Owner
- **Champion:** Sales Manager (day-to-day user who champions the solution)

- **Influencer:** IT/Operations manager (if dealership has one)

**Buying Behavior:**
- **Decision Cycle:** 2-4 weeks (fast for B2B)
- **Decision Process:** Demo → Trial/Pilot → Contract
- **Budget Authority:** Sales Manager can approve up to 10K NOK/month; owner approval for larger commitments
- **Buying Criteria:** ROI proof, ease of use, Norwegian support, automotive-specific features

## Validated Customer Insights

### Lead Source Distribution (Based on RSA Network Research)

**From Founder's Direct Knowledge:**

1. **Website Contact Forms: ~35% of leads**
   - Customer visits dealership website
   - Fills out "Contact us" or "Request quote" form
   - Includes: name, email, phone, vehicle interest, message
   - Current problem: Goes to generic email inbox, slow response

2. **Importer Portal Notifications: ~40% of leads**
   - Customer searches on Toyota.no, VW.no, Nissan.no, etc.
   - Selects specific vehicle from specific dealership
   - Submits interest form on importer site
   - Importer sends email notification to dealership
   - Current problem: Dealerships have to manually check email and respond

3. **Email Inquiries: ~15% of leads**
   - Customer emails directly (info@dealership.no)
   - Often from mobile phone while researching
   - Current problem: Mixed in with other emails, easy to miss

4. **Facebook Lead Ads: ~10% of leads**
   - Paid advertising with lead capture forms
   - Customer clicks ad, fills form without leaving Facebook
   - Current problem: Have to manually download leads from Facebook, slow process

5. **Instagram/WhatsApp: <5% of leads**
   - NOT a primary channel for Norwegian car dealerships
   - Only mentioned occasionally
   - **Decision: NOT part of MVP, can add later if validated**

### Validated Pain Points (From Direct Conversations)

**Pain #1: Response Speed (Validated with 100% of contacts)**
- ALL dealerships admit their response time is too slow
- Industry best practice: < 5 minutes
- Actual performance: 4+ hours average
- After-hours/weekend leads: 24-48 hours (completely unacceptable)
- Quote from contact: "We know we're losing deals because we don't respond fast enough, but we're too busy to fix it"

**Pain #2: Lead Capture Complexity (Validated with 80% of contacts)**
- Most dealerships check multiple places for leads
- No unified system to see all leads in one place
- Sales reps miss leads because they're checking the wrong channel
- Quote: "It's like whack-a-mole - leads pop up everywhere and we can't keep track"

**Pain #3: No Follow-Up System (Validated with 90% of contacts)**
- Most dealerships respond to first inquiry, then forget to follow up
- No automated sequences (Day 3, Day 7 follow-ups)
- Rely on sales rep discipline (inconsistent)
- Quote: "We lose deals not because customers aren't interested, but because we forget to stay in touch"

**Pain #4: Can't Measure Performance (Validated with 60% of contacts)**
- Many dealerships don't track lead-to-sale conversion
- Don't know which marketing channels work best
- Can't calculate ROI on advertising spend
- Quote: "We spend 80K/month on Facebook ads, but honestly don't know if it's worth it"

**Pain #5: Generic AI Responses (If No Inventory)**
- AI says "I'll check if we have that car" (useless, customer already knows this)
- Sales rep has to follow up with "yes we have it" (extra step)
- Customer doubts if dealership actually has the car
- Competitor with real-time inventory wins the sale

### What Dealerships DON'T Need (Validated "Non-Problems")

**❌ Complex Inventory Management**
- Most use importer's system (Toyota.no, VW.no) or Finn.no
- Don't want another inventory database to maintain
- **Decision: Inventory are only PARTIALY part of MVP for test drive bookings**

**❌ Accounting Integration**
- Have existing accounting software (Tripletex, Visma)
- Not a pain point dealerships mentioned
- **Decision: NOT part of MVP**

**❌ Service Appointment Booking**
- Different workflow than car sales
- Often handled by separate service department
- **Decision: Service scheduling NOT part of MVP**

**❌ Instagram/WhatsApp Messaging**
- Not validated as significant lead source
- Would add complexity without clear value
- **Decision: NOT part of MVP, can add later**

## Use Cases (Validated with Customers)

## Sales Objection Handling Guide

### Objection 1: "We already have a CRM"
**Response:** "That's great! Norvalt isn't replacing your CRM - it's filling the gap. Your CRM helps you manage relationships after first contact. Norvalt ensures you never miss that first contact by capturing and responding to leads 24/7, then sending them to your CRM. Think of it as the front door to your sales process."

### Objection 2: "Our sales team won't use new software"
**Response:** "I hear you - we built Norvalt specifically to be easy. Your sales reps don't need training. Norvalt works in the background, capturing leads and responding automatically. All your team does is respond to the *qualified* leads we surface in the dashboard. They'll love it because they're only talking to hot prospects, not chasing cold leads."

### Objection 3: "AI can't handle car sales conversations"
**Response:** "You're right that AI can't close the deal - and it shouldn't! Norvalt's AI handles the first 90 seconds: acknowledging the inquiry, confirming the car is available, and letting the customer know a real person will follow up. The AI doesn't negotiate price or make promises. It just ensures you never lose a lead to slow response time. Your sales team does what they do best: selling cars."

### Objection 4: "Too expensive for our budget"
**Response:** "Let's look at the numbers together. Norvalt costs 6,000 NOK per month. Your average profit per car is probably 30-50,000 NOK. If Norvalt helps you close just ONE additional car per month by responding faster and following up better, it pays for itself 5-8 times over. Can we agree that capturing leads you're currently losing is worth investigating?"

### Objection 5: "What if it breaks or stops working?"
**Response:** "Fair concern. Here's our guarantee: 99.5% uptime, monitored 24/7. If anything goes wrong, we're notified immediately and fix it. Plus, all leads are stored safely even if there's a temporary issue. You also have my personal phone number - I'm the founder and ex-RSA regional manager, so I understand your business. We're not some distant support team."

### Objection 6: "We need to think about it"
**Response:** "Absolutely, this is an important decision. While you're thinking, can I ask: how many leads did you get last month? [Let them answer] And how many do you think you lost because of slow response times or lack of follow-up? [Let them answer] That's [X] potential sales. Just this month, while we're discussing. What specific concerns should we address to help you make a confident decision?"
